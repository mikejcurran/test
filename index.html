RiverView

Leverage immersive virtual tours and professional geomorphic assessments for better river management and stakeholder engagement

Our Mission

We provide municipalities, consulting firms, and other organizations with immersive virtual tours that showcase environmental and geomorphic data. From basic tours to in-depth geomorphic assessments, we help you visualize, quantify, and understand the landscape. Our intuitive approach to environmental data visualization will help the public engage with their natural and recreation resources, help stakeholders and resource managers better understand these dynamic systems, and smooth collaboration on river projects by bringing an interactive model of the site to your desktop.

Our Services

Basic Tour

Includes 360-degree panoramas with basic navigation and information hotspots. The tour will include:

  • High-resolution Imagery
  • Map Overlay
  • Identification of Important Markers

Geomorphic Assessment

Comprehensive tours with professional geomorphic data integration and reporting. We can provide:

  • Channel Migration Zone Delineation
  • Channel Migration Assessment
  • Reach Delineation
  • Bank/Bed Characterization
  • Flood/Erosion Risk Assessment
  • Restoration Potential

Customized Tour

How else would you like to characterize your site? We can develop and integrate:

  • Bathymmetry
  • Drone Imagery
  • Historical Aerial Imagery
  • Historical Maps
  • Channel Profile
  • Digital Elevation Model
  • Relative Elevation Model
  • DEM Differencing
  • ...And More
